Cape Inc. Logo and Packaging Design

Project overview: This is a project that I did for my Graphic Design class. In this project, my job was to design a logo for a fictional seafood company called Cape Inc. and their shrimp packaging. The objectives of the logo were that it should look simple, modern, and professional, and it should have the vibe of the sea. The objectives of the shrimp packaging were that it should be eye-catching so that it could stand out when it is displayed in the market.
